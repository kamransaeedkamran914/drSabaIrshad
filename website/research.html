Research Focus

Exploring the Genetic Frontiers of Health and Disease

Rare Genetic Disorders

Investigation of the molecular basis of rare genetic conditions prevalent in the Pakistani population, utilizing advanced genomic sequencing techniques to identify novel mutations.

Neurodevelopmental Disorders

Research into the genetic complexities of neurodevelopmental conditions, aiming to understand the biological pathways involved in brain development and function.

Molecular Genetics of Deafness

Comprehensive study of families with hereditary hearing loss to map disease genes and understand the pathophysiology of deafness.

Genetic Counseling

Translating research findings into clinical practice by providing genetic counseling to affected families, helping them understand inheritance patterns and risks.

Biochemical Analysis

Conducting biochemical studies on nutritional status and environmental factors affecting health, including heavy metal analysis in food sources.
