Biography

Prof. Dr. Saba Irshad is a distinguished academic and researcher at the School of Biochemistry and Biotechnology, University of the Punjab, Lahore. With a profound commitment to advancing the field of genetics, she has made significant contributions to understanding rare genetic disorders within the Pakistani population.

She earned her Ph.D. in Biochemistry/Molecular Biology from Quaid-e-Azam University (QAU), Islamabad, and was awarded a Gold Medal during her M.Phil studies. To further specialize, she completed her Post-Doctorate at Columbia University, New York, USA, focusing on the molecular genetics of rare genetic disorders.

Dr. Irshad is an HEC Approved Supervisor who has mentored over 8 Ph.D. and 40 M.Phil scholars, guiding the next generation of scientists.
